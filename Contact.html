Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should my dog be groomed?

This depends on the breed, coat type, and lifestyle.
Most dogs benefit from grooming every 4–8 weeks to keep their coat healthy and free from matting.

2. Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Yes, appointments are recommended to ensure we can accommodate your preferred time.
Walk-ins may be accepted depending on availability.

3. What should I bring to my pet’s grooming appointment?

Please bring any special grooming instructions, your pet’s leash or carrier,
and any required medical information if your dog has allergies or conditions we should know about.

4. How long does a grooming session take?

It depends on the service and the dog’s size, coat, and temperament. Most appointments take between 1 to 3 hours.
We’ll give you an estimated time at drop-off.

5. What if my dog is nervous or aggressive?

We have experience working with nervous pets and will do our best to make them comfortable.
However, if a dog is aggressive, we may need to end the session for safety reasons.
