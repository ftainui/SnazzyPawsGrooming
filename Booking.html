Owner and Pet Information

Booking information

Toileting

Please ensure your pet has had a toilet break before arriving to help them stay comfortable.

Vaccinations

All pets must be up to date with their vaccinations for the safety of all animals.

Health Concerns

Inform us of any medical conditions, sensitivities, or recent injuries in advance.

Matting

Severely matted coats may need shaving or extra grooming time. Additional charges may apply.

Drop-off and Pickup

Be on time for both drop-off and pickup to keep appointments running smoothly.

Cancellations

Please provide at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or changes to your appointment.

Aggressive Behavior

We may refuse service to pets that display aggressive or dangerous behavior.

Special Requests

Mention any grooming preferences or styles at the time of booking or drop-off.
